\textbf{What this guide shows}

With \texttt{authority-en}, the page gains a restrained green header, a structured reference panel on the right, and the authority attachment defaults. The commands above demonstrate how identifiers, customer numbers, and dates enter that panel. Fields left empty simply disappear.

For your own document, keep the Form B selection and adjust the recipient, subject, reference values, and attachments in \texttt{content.tex}. Reserve \texttt{LetterBody} for the written content. All values shown here are fictional and neutralized; the layout is DIN 5008 Form B oriented rather than certified.
