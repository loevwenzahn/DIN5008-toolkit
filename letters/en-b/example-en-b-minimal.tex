\begin{LetterBody}

\textbf{What this guide shows}

When the additional panels would be unnecessary, \texttt{minimal-en} keeps the Form B page deliberately quiet. A black monogram remains, while decorative color blocks and the automatic attachment list stay out of view.

Set the recipient and subject in \texttt{content.tex}; add the short correspondence inside \texttt{LetterBody}. Sender information continues to come from \texttt{data/sender-data.tex}. If unchanged demo data remains, the toolkit prints a compiler-log reminder before final use, not a visible warning in the PDF. The preset offers a DIN 5008 Form B oriented starting point without a certification claim.

\end{LetterBody}
